---
title: "First Workflow"
description: "Learn how to download models and custom nodes"
---

1. Download a checkpoint file. Maybe [Stable Diffusion v1.5](https://huggingface.co/runwayml/stable-diffusion-v1-5/resolve/main/v1-5-pruned-emaonly.ckpt). Place the file under `ComfyUI/models/checkpoints`.

1. Refresh the ComfyUI.

1. Click `Load Default` button to use the default workflow.

1. In the `Load Checkpoint` node, select the checkpoint file you just downloaded.

1. Click `Queue Prompt` and watch your image generated. Play around with the prompts to generate different images.
